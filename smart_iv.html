Objective

Introduction
Mar 2020 - Dec 2021

Intravenous (IV) infusion therapy is a traditional medical procedure that is applied in all branches of medicine and involves the insertion of fluid directly into a patient’s vein using a needle. The most common reasons for receiving IV infusion are replacement of lost fluid and electrolytes; for treating dehydration; for chemotherapy, anaesthesia, or drugs that the patient cannot receive orally; for transfusion of blood or blood plasma during or after surgery as a result of heavy bleeding; and for the intake of vitamins, minerals, and, today, increasingly in the treatment of patients with coronavirus disease.

Problem Statement

During our hospital visits, we see that the Corona patient is too much and doctors and nurses are very few. The nurse cannot sit with the Corona patient for long. When the nurse leaves the patient with drip. We see that when the fluid inside the drip becomes completely empty and the doctors/nurses could not reach the Corona patient timely to remove the needle from the veins , the pressure inside the veins overcomes the pressure inside the IV, allowing the blood to flow back into the line which is not desired. This causes harm to the patient.

Proposed Solution

The smart IV dosing system designed by us has taken into account, complex problems that includes different elements such as detection of fluid level in the IV bottle, signalling/alarms, regulation and closure of infusion flow, remote location IV therapy monitoring, patient’s database, the efficiency of medical staff, and medical staff response time to alarms.

We have designed a system Robotic Smart IV Stand based on IOT & Sensors. As our experimental results dedicated to the design showed that the novel designed pole mobilization can be easily improved by the use of Load Cell, Node MCU, Arduino UNO, etc.

Our literature search identified a significant number of papers focusing on solving each of these individual problems. But, we have solved all these problems and have designed a single system which makes our project innovative.

Our Idea Smart Intravenous Stand With Real Time Drip Monitoring & Alert System is a part of a INTELLECTULE PROPERTY INDIA. The Patent for our Idea has been made published. Our Indian Patent Application Number is 202111017176.
